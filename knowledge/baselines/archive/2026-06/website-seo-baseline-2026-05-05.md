2. Homepage Review

Confirmed:

Title shown in search results: Home - Grizzly Electrical Solutions.

The header includes the phone number, a DFW-wide service statement, and the Rowlett address.

The main visible headline theme is We Strive To Offer Quality Work In A Timely Manner, At An Affordable Cost.

The page uses trust elements including review snippets, licensed and insured messaging, free phone estimates, discounts, and emergency-service messaging.

Internal links exist to the four main service pages, finance, service areas, reviews, gallery, blog, and contact.

A contact/inquiry form block is present near the bottom of the page.

A visible Error: Contact form not found. message appears on the homepage.

What is working:

Strong phone visibility.

Clear local business identity with address, hours, and service-area statement.

Existing trust signals are easy to spot.

Navigation is simple and easy to understand.

What is weak:

The page title is generic and misses local/service intent.

The homepage does not lead with a clear primary H1-style promise such as DFW electrician, troubleshooting, panel work, or EV charger installation.

The repeated hero copy makes the page feel broken or cluttered in public HTML output.

The core services section is too broad for SEO and under-represents priority services like troubleshooting, recessed lighting, service upgrades, and generator inlet/interlock work.

Internal links push users only into four broad service pages rather than the priority service set.

The main CTA language Book online is generic for an electrician; it does not reinforce emergency help, troubleshooting, estimate requests, or service-specific actions.

Review copy includes typos and one duplicated testimonial, which weakens trust.

Mobile readability:

Needs verification with device screenshots.

The repeated hero blocks and long review sections are likely creating unnecessary scroll length.

Biggest homepage opportunities:

Replace the generic homepage title and hero copy with a DFW electrical-services positioning statement.

Surface direct links to troubleshooting, recessed lighting, panel replacement, service upgrades, EV charger installation, and generator-related services.

Remove or repair the broken form output and tighten the bottom-of-page CTA.
